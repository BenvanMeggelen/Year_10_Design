The reason why I like League of Legends

League of Legends is a great game to play with friends, it is a 5vs5 game or if you like to play with a smaller group of friends, you can also play 3vs3. The game is free to play and has 153 champions available. It is a highly interactive, complex game and has a lot of different varieties on how to play it. The usual game takes about 30 minutes, so it can be a fun evening with a lot of games or a great study break to take your mind off school. The game is a real-time multiplayer strategy pc game, which means that you have to focus on it.

Health benefits of Gaming

Research found out that there are indeed also positive health aspects linked to gaming. Playing real time strategy games like League of Legends a lot increases the players ability to process information faster than other people. They are usually better at being able to think clearly under pressure, which can be very beneficial for students. For introverted kids it can also be a way to make new friends and improve social skills, and to have a safe environment where they feel good after school. It also improves their ability to visually imagine something, which can help in areas like art, or being creative. Long time players of these skills also usually have a faster reaction time, which can be helpful in various other situations as well. Sometimes it can even have the potential to save a life, like avoiding a car crash.
